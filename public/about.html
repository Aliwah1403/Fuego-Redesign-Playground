About Us

FuegoxRelvetti is a local(Kenyan) streetwear brand that focuses on creating clothing products. Relvetti means gradually growing to greater heights. We describe our products as fashionable, elegant, comfortable but at the same time affordable. When making the products we use high quality fabrics which are durable. We so far concentrate on merging the fashion industry together with media as that can be seen in our social media. Through visuals we try to bring our concepts and ideas to life and also to our consumers it aids in understanding our content and designs while being able to convey important messages.
